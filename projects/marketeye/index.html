MarketEye

Product Link : MarketEye

MarketEye helps you manage your investments effortlessly by watching the market for you in real time and assists you in taking action in a timely manner.

Price Drop Alert

Busy professionals often pick good stocks but fail to exit on time because they’re too busy on their day job. Price drop alert prevents profits from turning into losses by alerting you when you need to exit.

  • Setting up alerts is a 2-click process
  • Trailing the price on the way up lets users keep more of their profits
  • Exiting on time allows users to have some cash to buy the dip or other opportunities at reasonable valuations
  • Notifications are supported on Email and Whatsapp
